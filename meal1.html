Beef Stew and Rice

Ingredients

  • 500g beef stew meat (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 carrots (sliced)
  • 2 potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped or blended)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef broth or water
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or mixed herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander or parsley (for garnish)
  • 2 cups cooked white rice (served on the side)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and brown the beef on all sides.
  3. Remove beef, add onions and sauté until soft.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, cook briefly.
  5. Stir in tomatoes and tomato paste, cook until thickened.
  6. Return beef to pot and mix well.
  1. Add carrots, potatoes, and bell pepper.
  2. Pour in broth or water, stir and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until beef is tender (45–60 mins).
  4. Adjust seasoning with salt and herbs.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander or parsley.
  6. Serve hot with cooked white rice.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: ~780 kcal
  • Protein: ~47g
  • Carbohydrates: ~55g
  • Fat: ~35g
  • Serving Size: 200g cooked rice + 300g beef stew
